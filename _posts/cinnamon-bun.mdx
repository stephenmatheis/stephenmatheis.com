If you enjoy [cozy fantasy stories](https://www.reddit.com/r/CozyFantasy/) and [LitRPGs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LitRPG) then you might like [RavensDagger's Cinnamon Bun](https://www.royalroad.com/fiction/31429/cinnamon-bun).<Sup>1</Sup>

I can't put it down. Thank goodness the [author says the story isn't meant to end!](https://www.reddit.com/r/litrpg/comments/13vo298/comment/jm9x4xp/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=web2x&context=3)!
